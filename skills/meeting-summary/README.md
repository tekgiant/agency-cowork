# Meeting Summary

Generate executive meeting summaries from Teams meeting recaps and transcripts. Produces structured notes with key decisions, action items, and follow-ups organized by attendee.

## Features

- Teams meeting recap extraction
- Transcript processing and analysis
- Executive summary generation
- Key decision extraction and highlighting
- Action item tracking with owners
- Attendee-based organization of discussion points
- Follow-up item identification

## Usage

Trigger phrases: "summarize a meeting", "create meeting notes", "get meeting recap", "write meeting summary", "what happened in the meeting".

Examples:
- "Summarize my last meeting"
- "Create meeting notes for the design review"
- "What were the action items from today's standup?"

## Prerequisites

- Microsoft Teams MCP configured
- Meeting recap or transcript available in Teams
- Access to the target meeting's content
